Getting Started

1 Launch the App

Click the button below to open the music learning application

2 Choose a Lesson

Select from available lessons (Sarali Varisai, Jantai, or Advanced Patterns)

3 Hit Play

Click the Green Play button to start the lesson. Watch the animation and listen!

4 Control Your Learning

Toggle Sound and Voice as needed. Use Stop to pause at any time

5 Practice

Repeat lessons as many times as you need. Try singing along with the voice!

🎵 LAUNCH MUSIC NOTES APP 🎵

What Each Control Does

▶ Play Button - Starts playing the lesson. Watch the notes light up!

⏹ Stop Button - Stops playback and resets to the beginning

🔊 Sound Toggle - Play/Mute musical notes

🎤 Voice Toggle - Play/Mute voice pronunciation

← Back Button - Return to lesson selection (on lesson page)

Tips for Learning

  • Start with Sarali Varisai - the foundational lesson
  • Enable both Sound and Voice for best learning
  • Play each lesson multiple times until you memorize it
  • Try singing along with the voice pronunciation
  • Progress to Advanced Patterns once you master the basics
  • Be patient - learning music takes time and practice

Adding Your Own Lessons

You can easily add custom lessons by creating text files in the lessons/ folder:

Create: lessons/my-lesson.txt
Content: S R G M P D N Ṡ
(One pattern per line, notes separated by spaces)

The app will automatically detect and create a button for your new lesson!
